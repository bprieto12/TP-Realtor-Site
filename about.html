about
     Tami began her real estate career as an escrow assistant in 1989 and ultimately became an Escrow Officer. After 25 years in escrow, Tami realized that, rather than sitting behind a desk, she needed to serve her community and directly support her clientele. Based on her knowledge Tami can help her clients navigate the sometimes complicated steps of the real estate process. Whether you are buying or selling your home, Tami can make the process much simpler because she can foresee and fix issues before the close of escrow. Tami has lived in Orange County her entire life. She and her husband Bryan raised six children together in the City of Fountain Valley. In their down time they enjoy hiking and spending time with their family and friends.
